SuperNova Quiz

'SuperNova Quiz' is a trivia-like game of six rounds with seven questions in each one.

  • Learn more
  • Join us

A large share of the rounds are visual and audial, both hand-crafted and AI-made.

While some knowledge is required to play, unlike in a typical trivia, you will be mostly relying on your logical reasoning, wit, creativity and teamwork to win.

We believe this makes for more engaging, interactive and inclusive experience.

We aspire to turn 'SuperNova Quiz' into a nation- and worldwide network of leagues, where teams would be able to compete against each other.

Our answer-accepting and answer-checking software greatly decreases the burden on organizers and prevents wasting paper.

While 6 rounds with 7 questions is the standard, we provide flexibility to organizers and are able to provide any number of rounds and questions, as well as rounds on specific topics requested by organizers.

To introduce dynamism into the game, we offer a wide variety of rulesets for rounds. This requires the teams to find a distinct strategy for each game.

Our questions are very hard to answer by just googling them. This provides an additional layer of protection against cheating.
